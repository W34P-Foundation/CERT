STEM Updates & Announcements

Supporting technology education and community initiatives

Technology Fee Support

W34P Foundation provided technology fee scholarships to 42 high school students, removing financial barriers to STEM education access. We are committed to ensuring that economic circumstances do not limit educational opportunity.

By providing this support, we empower students to pursue STEM courses and develop the technical skills necessary for success in today's technology-driven world.

High School Band Support

W34P Foundation supports the local high school band by providing meals before games and performances. We recognize the dedication and commitment of our student musicians.

Our volunteers ensure that band members have the nutrition and support they need to perform at their best.
